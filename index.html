B's Homemade Ice Cream Ltd is a family run business, serving delicious frozen treats to the people of Trinidad and Tobago for over 25 years. The company is renowned for its homemade ice-cream, reminiscent of the kind grandmothers all over the country used to make. B's serves up a variety of delicious traditional flavours such as vanilla, chocolate, rum and raisin and pistachio, as well as exotic tropical flavours such as coconut, soursop, sorrel, pumpkin and banana to name a few.
